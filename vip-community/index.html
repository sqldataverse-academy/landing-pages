Community Culture

A few things that make this space worth being in.

01

No selling, ever.

This community exists for one reason — to help everyone here learn SQL and complete the course. No affiliate links. No upsells. No "DM me for my course." If it happens, it's gone.

02

Doubts get answered.

No question is too basic. No one gets mocked for not knowing something. The whole point of a shared learning space is that your gap is someone else's answer, and vice versa. Ask clearly, and someone will help.

03

Attempt before you ask.

When you post a doubt, share what you tried first — even if it's wrong. This isn't gatekeeping; it helps the person answering understand where your thinking broke down, which means a better answer for you.

04

Wins count, no matter how small.

Finished M1? Post it. Wrote your first window function without copying the syntax? Post it. Solved a Weekly Problem in under 10 minutes? Post it. This channel runs on real moments from the journey, not just final achievements.

05

Be the person you'd want as a community member.

Answer when you know the answer. Share resources that helped you. Welcome new members. The community is worth exactly as much as the people in it choose to make it — and that includes you.
